Christmas Special

Dave the brave on fire

Friday December 25th

A chic and daring 1920's fire show with acrobatics, audacious fire stunts, gangster pizazz and a blast of mojo that will have you screaming!This is an elegant, powerful yet playfully upbeat act with world class fire prop manipulation and unique effects.

Dave the brave and his group consists of some of Sweden's most established fire artists who work in close collaboration with professional circus acts from different parts of the world. With precision and feeling, they invite audiences into their sparkling world, which is magical, beautiful and breathtaking! With over 10 years of experience performing to huge crowds all over the world, the performers offer a range of customised packages for all events and budgets. From intimate and dynamic solo acts, duo shows and explosive fire productions synchronised with live musicians, lighting effects and a range of circus performers.

Harry Houdini

An exclusive night, a unique experience, a magical event. Meet the one and only Harry Houdini, coming to Sweden for the first time to perform in Cirus Vegan!

AcroFamilj

Satruday December 26th

The nation-wide famous AcroFamilj is back to circus Vegan. The highly-trained Andersson family acrobats, known as AcroFamilj will show us their latest life-daring tricks. Action packed with outstanding feats, beautiful choreography, color and excitement — the show is one of the favorite nightlife performances in Sweden.

From contortionist acts to high wire stunts, this performance has it all. Now is your chance to witness the most extreme acrobat skill in China, at the theater known to be the best acrobatic show in Beijing.

A night with laughter and fun

YoYo The Clown

Saturday January 9th

Sweden's funniest clown will come to Circus Vegan on January the 8th. A promise night full of laughter and joy for both kids and grow-ups!

Yoyo's show is packed full of rib tickling fun & tricks that will have kids and mums and dads in stitches! Children & families love to see funny business, Unicycles, magical tricks, juggling, colourful hat tricks, balancing rola-rola, lots of ”kiddy Comedy” party games, the latest pop tunes & balloons of course.
